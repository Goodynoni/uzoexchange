Security

Learn how UzoExchange secures your account, funds, and transactions.

Security Commitment

Account Protection

Enable two-factor authentication and a strong password to maximize account security. We never request your password by email or phone, so be cautious of phishing attempts.

Encryption & Infrastructure

All sensitive information is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols. Our infrastructure uses hardened servers, firewalls, and routine patching to keep systems secure.

Threat Monitoring

Our team continuously monitors transactions and system activity for suspicious behavior. We act quickly to block threats and investigate any potential security incidents.

Trusted Storage

We store digital assets with secure wallet controls and deploy cold storage when appropriate. Our custody practices are designed to protect funds from unauthorized access.
