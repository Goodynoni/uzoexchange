Privacy Policy Overview

What We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to verify your identity, fulfill transactions, and protect your account. This includes contact details, identity documents, wallet addresses, and transaction history.

How We Use It

Your data is used to process trades, improve platform performance, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations. We do not use your personal information for unrelated commercial purposes without your consent.

How We Protect It

We use encryption, secure access controls, and routine security audits to protect your data. Only authorized team members and vetted service providers may access your information when necessary.

Sharing & Your Rights

We disclose data only to trusted partners and regulators when required by law or to provide our services. You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your information subject to applicable retention rules.
