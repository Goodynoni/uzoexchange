Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about trading, accounts, and security.

How do I create an account?

Click Register, complete the signup form, and verify your email to start trading.

What payment methods are accepted?

We support bank transfers, debit/credit cards, and select digital wallets depending on your region.

How safe is my account?

Enable two-factor authentication and use a strong password for the best protection.

How do I withdraw funds?

Go to your wallet, choose Withdraw, and follow the prompts to transfer funds securely.
